Philosophy

What "nominal"
actually means.

In engineering, nominal means performing as designed. No overengineering. No filler. Just exactly what the system needs to work.

That's the standard every resource here is held to. If it doesn't make you faster or more confident on an exam, it doesn't ship.

No fluff. No 200-page textbook summaries. Just the load-bearing content — organized so you can actually use it.
